How It Works

Two clear journeys, one connected platform.

Teacher Journey

  1. Subscribe to a package based on your student count.
  2. Access your private dashboard immediately.
  3. Generate student accounts within your package limit.
  4. Upload lectures, videos, exams, and assignments.
  5. Track engagement and results from one place.

Student Journey

  1. Receive the account from the teacher.
  2. Log in and see only that teacher's content.
  3. Watch recorded lectures and course videos.
  4. Complete assignments and join exams.
  5. Follow progress and stay on schedule.
FAQ

Questions teachers usually ask before getting started.

Do I need to build my own website first?

No. EduPlatform already gives you the ready learning environment, student access, content delivery, and exam flow.

Can I control how many students join?

Yes. Your package defines the number of student accounts you can generate and manage.

What can students access after login?

They can watch lessons, open recordings, solve assignments, and take exams related to their teacher only.
